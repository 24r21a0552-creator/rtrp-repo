History

MLRIT Sports has a rich history of promoting excellence in athletics and sportsmanship. Over the years, our students have excelled in numerous intercollegiate and state-level competitions, setting benchmarks in various sports.

To foster holistic development through sports and physical activities, empowering students to achieve excellence in academics and athletics alike.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution for sports and athletics, nurturing future champions who excel on and off the field.
